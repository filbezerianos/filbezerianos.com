Behind the Scenes

Passionate about solving complex challenges and empowering teams to reach their full potential.

Oct 30, 2024 - 2 minute read

As a professional I stand at the intersection of technology and business, specialising in designing, developing and implementing solutions to drive tangible business outcomes. I work with industry-leading enterprises and exceptionally talented individuals to transform technical potential into substantial business value and reshaping entire organisations.

I am deeply committed to continuous learning - for a list of courses and certifications visit Credentials, my Credly profile or LinkedIn. I strongly believe that technology is not a standalone solution in itself, but a component of a broader strategy, that includes its integration within the organisation and enhancing user experience. This belief drives my effort to stay up to date with technological advancements, understand business and market shifts and delve into user experience and technology adoption. Through this blog, I aim to share valuable perspectives and insights across these topics, which also helps me organise my thoughts and deepen my understanding.

My career journey begun as a Software Engineer, where I developed big data solutions focused on real-time analysis and predictive modelling within the Oil and Pharmaceutical sectors. I soon discovered my passion for not just developing, but also seeing the business impact of my work. This steered my career toward technical client-facing roles in the FinTech and Cloud industries, where I have worked as a Solutions Architect and Technical Lead, managing and guiding cross-functional technical and business teams in delivering solutions that significantly impacted our clients.

Currently, I am working in Product and Technology Strategy domain, leading large engagements focusing on Cloud, Data and AI solutions, across industries, including Banking & Finance, Life Sciences, Media and Public Sector.

Outside of my professional life, I am exploring and appreciate the natural world and engaging with different mindsets and cultures, striving to understand what drives them.
